About Cristano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo (CR7) is a Portuguese footballer widely considered one of the greatest players of all time. He has won five Ballon d'Or awards and is the all-time top scorer in men's international football with 141 goals and 223 international appearances.

Ronaldo has played for several top European clubs, including Sporting CP, Manchester United (twice), Real Madrid, and Juventus. He is known for his goal-scoring ability, speed, powerful shots, headers, and dribbling skills. He has also had success with the Portuguese national team, leading them to a victory in the 2016 UEFA European Championship. After his European career, he joined the Saudi Arabian club Al-Nassr FC. Ronaldo's career achievements also include five UEFA Champions League titles and being the first player to score 900 career goals in official matches.

His achivements

  • Five Ballon d'Or awards
  • All-time top scorer in men's international football
  • Five UEFA Champions League titles
  • First player to score 900 career goals in official matches
  • Winner of the 2016 UEFA European Championship
  • Winner of the 2014 FIFA World Cup
